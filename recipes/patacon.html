Patacón

Ingredientes

  • 2 green plantains
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste

Instrucciones

  1. Peel the plantains and cut them into 2-inch pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Fry the plantain pieces until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Remove the plantains from the oil and drain on paper towels. While still warm, flatten each piece with a fork or a tostonera.
  4. Return the flattened plantains to the hot oil and fry again until crispy, about 2-3 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels and sprinkle with salt.
