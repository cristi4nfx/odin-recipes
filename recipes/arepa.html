Arepa

Ingredientes

  • 2 cups masa harina
  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instrucciones

  1. In a large bowl, combine masa harina and salt. Gradually add warm water, mixing with your hands until a dough forms. Let rest for 10 minutes.
  2. Divide dough into 8 equal portions and shape each into a ball. Flatten each ball into a disk about ½ inch thick.
  3. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook arepas for 5-7 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through.
